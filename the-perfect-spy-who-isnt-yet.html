The Perfect Spy Who Isn’t Yet

Why the perfect spy is the one who can’t lie, can’t be turned, can’t be bought — and why the system passes on him now.

The author holds no security clearance and is not an intelligence officer. This is a joke. It is satire. The only thing real here is the character it describes.

Field Assessment SUBJECT: McKendry, Sean  ·  PROFILE: the perfect un-liar  ·  STATUS: not selected — yet
GOVERNING LAW OF THIS FORUM: only humans can score.

The perfect spy is supposed to be the perfect liar. The subject is the perfect spy because he is the perfect un-liar. There is no cover to crack, because there is nothing under the cover but the truth. You cannot blow a cover that was never there.

1 · No tell

A polygraph reads the gap between what a person says and what’s true. Run it on a man whose doctrine is NEVER LIE and you get a very boring afternoon — a flat line, because there’s no gap to find. The machine gets more confident when it’s lying; humans hedge when unsure; the subject does neither, because nothing is ever being hidden. Unreadable by being completely readable.

2 · Un-turnable

Every recruitment, every flip, every piece of kompromat needs one thing: a secret to hold over you. The subject keeps the record visible — “the record is kept; you are not.” There is nothing to hold. You can’t blackmail a man who already published the file.

3 · Un-buyable

The craft’s deepest fear is the asset who can be purchased by the other side. Gold is earned; gray is bought; the subject’s say has no price. The other side could back a truck up to his door and leave with nothing but a receipt for a $1 donation to a kids’ camp.

4 · The verdict, and the joke

The system that can’t grant him clearance — because a clearance is discretionary and won’t open by petition — is the exact system that would most want the one operative who can’t be read, can’t be turned, can’t be bought. They’d pass on him now for the very traits that make him the only one you could ever fully trust. That’s why the future laughs: history will read the file and see they declined the unbreakable one on a technicality. The cleanest asset, filed under “not selected.”

The machine can draft the legend.
Only a human can be one. 🕵️
