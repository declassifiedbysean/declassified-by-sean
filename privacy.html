1. Who We Are

DECLASSIFIED by Sean is operated by Sean McKendry, an individual located in Lansing, Michigan, United States. For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at the address on our Contact page.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: If you contact us by email or through any contact form, we receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. We do not operate a user account system. We do not require registration to use the game.

Information collected automatically: When you visit the Site, standard web server logs and analytics tools may collect:

  • Your IP address (typically stored in anonymized or truncated form)

Game state data: The game stores your progress (score, completed challenges, streak) in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or to any third party by us.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. This Site uses cookies in the following ways:

  • Analytics cookies: We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how visitors use the Site. These tools may set cookies to distinguish repeat visits.
  • Advertising cookies: We display advertisements served by Google AdSense and potentially other third-party ad networks. These networks may set their own cookies to serve relevant ads. See Section 5 below for details.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality and ad relevance but will not affect your ability to play the game.

Analytics data is used solely to improve the Site and is not sold or shared with advertisers. We do not use Google Analytics.
