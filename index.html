DECLASSIFIED

Fact-check six acts of documented claims (2015–2026). Assess evidence, identify rhetorical patterns, learn how misinformation spreads.

DECLASSIFIED is a free, open-access fact-checking game series. No signup. No ads inside games. Each act covers a specific period, introduces new rhetorical patterns, and builds on previous learning. Start with Act I (2015–2016 campaign) or jump to any act.

How it works: Read a claim from the documented record. Assess whether it's TRUE, FALSE, MISLEADING, or INSUFFICIENT. Choose the evidence tier. Identify the rhetorical pattern. Get immediate feedback on your analysis. Your score persists across acts.
