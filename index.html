How it works

Read a claim from the documented record. Assess whether it's TRUE, FALSE, MISLEADING, or INSUFFICIENT. Choose the evidence tier. Identify the rhetorical pattern. Get immediate feedback on your analysis. Your score persists across acts.

Current Events: MAHA, GradedLesson Mode: Make America Healthy Again, claim by claim — what the science backs (ultra-processed foods) vs. debunked detours (vaccine-autism, fluoride), and the report caught faking its citations. Sincerity isn’t evidence.
